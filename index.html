GP Resilience Training Decision Aid

Select your stress level, preferred format and boundary-setting focus. Then click Get Recommendation.

How to use

  1. Choose your current stress level.
  2. Select the training format you prefer.
  3. Pick which boundary‑setting topic is most relevant.
  4. Click “Get Recommendation” to see:
    • A customised text recommendation.
    • A bar chart comparing baseline vs. post‑training intention‑to‑leave rates for a simulated workshop matching your choices.

What the chart means

The blue bar shows the % of GPs who would intend to leave before the training; the green bar shows the % who intend to leave after. A larger drop indicates greater effectiveness of that intervention in retaining staff.
